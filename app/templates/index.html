Select panels for GDS software interviews

It's important that panels are made up of people from diverse backgrounds. This helps counteract unconscious bias, and shows candidates that GDS is a diverse organisation. It's a small thing, but it's part of our strategy for addressing the lack of diversity in our communities (particularly in the software development community).

Staffing panels fairly can be a little bit tricky though. If we're not careful, we could end up asking the same few women of colour to do the lion's share of the interviewing.

This application uses an algorithm to choose panels that meet our diversity requirements, while constraining the number of interviews that each individual needs to do.

Select an interview panel

To select a panel, you'll need to provide a list of people who have volunteered to sit on panels.

The GDS recruitment team should be able to give you a list of the people who've completed the interview, including their diversity information.

Use this tool by creating a spreadsheet with details of the candidates and the pool of panelists. Paste the values from your spreadsheet, and this tool will select some panels for you.
